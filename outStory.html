Our Story

Our Heritage

Rice of Kashmir is rooted in the heart of Azad Kashmir, near the revered shrine of Mian Muhammad Baksh. Our rice is cultivated with the utmost care and dedication, reflecting the rich cultural and spiritual legacy of the region

Why Choose Us

By choosing Rice of Kashmir, you are not only savoring premium quality rice but also supporting sustainable farming practices and the local community.

Our Journey

For generations, our farmers have perfected the art of rice cultivation, resulting in the exceptional quality of our Super Kainaat 1121 Basmati Rice. Each grain is a testament to our commitment to excellence and tradition.
